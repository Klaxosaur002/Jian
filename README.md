# Jian [鹣]

The Jian, also known as birds that fly together [比翼鳥], are mythical birds with only one eye and one wing. They are born imperfect and must lean on each other to fly. Their mutual dependence is eternal, lasting through death and rebirth.

(Reference: [Darling in the Franxx Wiki](https://darling-in-the-franxx.fandom.com/wiki/Jian))

## About Jian

Jian is a project dedicated to defeating silence and suppression. It has **two main components**:

### 1. Public Pastebin

* Inspired by the **Uncensored Library** in Minecraft by Reporters Without Borders and BlockWorks.
* Serves as a **public repository for information**, enabling free access to content that might otherwise be suppressed.
* Currently **centralized** due to storage requirements, but content will be **encrypted** to maintain privacy.

### 2. Temporary Local Notes (Tails-Like Mode)

* Functions like a **temporary, local-only OS** for sensitive notes.
* Saves content in **RAM or on a USB**, ensuring it is **erased on program exit**.
* Focused on privacy and **ephemeral data storage**, preventing permanent traces.
